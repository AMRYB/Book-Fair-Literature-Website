Frequently Asked Questions

How often does the book club meet?

We typically meet once a month for our main discussion. However, we also have forum discussions ongoing throughout the month where members can participate at their own pace. Special events like author visits are scheduled occasionally as opportunities arise.

Do I need to finish the book before attending?

While it's ideal to complete the book before the main discussion, we understand that not everyone reads at the same pace. We have separate forum discussions for different sections of the book, so you can participate in discussions for the parts you've completed. We just ask that you're mindful of spoilers in the main discussion.

How are books selected for the club?

Our book selection process is democratic! Club members can nominate books, and then we vote on the selections. We try to vary our reading list to include different genres, cultures, and perspectives. We also occasionally select books that coincide with author events or literary festivals in our area.

Is there a membership fee?

There is no fee to join our book club! The only cost is purchasing or borrowing the books we read. We often partner with local libraries and bookstores to ensure members can access our selections affordably. For in-person meetings, we sometimes collect a small contribution for refreshments.

Can I join discussions online if I can't attend in person?

Absolutely! We have a hybrid format that accommodates both in-person and virtual participation. All our main discussions have a Zoom option, and our online forums are active 24/7. Many of our members participate exclusively online, while others enjoy the mix of virtual and in-person engagement.
