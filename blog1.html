Why You Should Buy Wahat El-Yakoub ?!

New Release by Asir Books Publishing

Recently released by Asir Books Publishing, "Wahat El-Yakoub" is a new novel by Dr. Amr Abdel Hamid, the author of the bestselling series "Ard Zykola." This novel presents a fresh addition to the fantasy genre that Abdel Hamid is known for, but this time with an element of horror that will take the reader on an extraordinary journey. If you're a fan of fantasy and horror, "Wahat El-Yakoub" is a must-have for your collection.

A Glimpse into the Novel

The story is set in the heart of a vast desert, where a blink of the eye means death, and the inhabitants of the oasis live a life entwined with fear and legends. The novel delves into the mysterious "Children of the Well" and the secret of the guardian jinn that looms over the story. It's an epic journey that mixes fear, mythology, and challenge, all leading to an inevitable fate. The question is: Will you dare enter Wahat El-Yakoub?

The Author and His Unique Style

Dr. Amr Abdel Hamid is the renowned Egyptian author known for his success with the "Ard Zykola" series, which proved his ability to craft new and unusual worlds that blend fantasy with reality in an engaging way. In "Wahat El-Yakoub," Abdel Hamid brings his signature fantasy style, but with an added element of horror, making it a thrilling read for fans of both genres.

Content and the Challenges It Presents

When the novel says that "a blink of the eye means death," it sets the tone of the terrifying atmosphere that fills the oasis. The plot is filled with suspense and mystery, making readers eager to continue reading to uncover what happens next. The Children of the Well, who are central to the plot, will add another layer of intrigue and fear, while the guardian jinn remains an enigma, leading to a captivating unraveling of secrets as the story progresses.

Why You Should Buy the Novel

1. Diverse Literary Elements: If you love fantasy and horror, this novel gives you a unique blend of both genres.
2. A Unique Writing Style: The author is known for creating engaging adventures and new worlds that combine mythology and suspense in a captivating way.
3. A Fresh Twist: The novel combines elements of fantasy with horror, making it stand out from typical fantasy novels.
4. Wide Availability: Besides the Egyptian edition, the novel will also be available in other Arab countries, making it easily accessible.
